# Cost & ROI Template

Use this structure for "Cost & ROI" pillar articles.

## Article Structure

### TL;DR (2-3 sentences)
- Key cost figure
- ROI timeline
- Value proposition

### Main Content

#### H2: How Much Does [Topic] Cost?
- Open with a clear price range
- Break down by tier/type
- Include what's included at each level

#### H2: Cost Breakdown

##### H3: One-Time Costs
| Item | Cost Range | Notes |
|------|------------|-------|
| Setup | $X - $Y | What's included |
| Training | $X - $Y | Optional/Required |
| Integration | $X - $Y | Complexity factors |

##### H3: Ongoing Costs
| Item | Monthly | Annual | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| Subscription | $X | $Y | Per user/flat |
| Maintenance | $X | $Y | Included/Extra |
| Support | $X | $Y | Basic/Premium |

#### H2: Hidden Costs to Watch For
- Numbered list of 5-7 hidden costs
- How to avoid them
- Questions to ask vendors

#### H2: ROI Calculator

##### H3: Investment
- Total first-year cost formula
- Ongoing annual cost

##### H3: Returns
- Time saved: X hours/week × $rate
- Revenue increase: %
- Cost reduction: $

##### H3: Payback Period
- Formula: Total Investment / Monthly Benefit
- Typical timeline
- Factors affecting speed

#### H2: Cost Comparison: Build vs Buy

| Factor | Build In-House | Buy Solution |
|--------|----------------|--------------|
| Initial Cost | $X | $Y |
| Time to Deploy | X months | Y weeks |
| Maintenance | Your team | Vendor |
| Customization | Full | Limited |
| Risk | Higher | Lower |

#### H2: Is It Worth It?
- Break-even analysis
- Long-term value
- Intangible benefits
- Risk assessment

#### H2: How to Reduce Costs
- 5-7 actionable tips
- Negotiation strategies
- Timing considerations

### FAQ Section (5-8 questions)
Cost-focused questions:
- "How much does [topic] cost per month?"
- "What is the cheapest [topic] option?"
- "Is there a free version of [topic]?"
- "What is the ROI of [topic]?"
- "How long until [topic] pays for itself?"
- "What hidden costs should I watch for?"

### Meta Description
- Under 160 characters
- Include price range
- Mention ROI or value

## Writing Style
- Be specific with numbers
- Use ranges when exact varies
- Include source references
- Update regularly
- Be transparent about assumptions

## SEO Guidelines
- Target "how much does [X] cost" keywords
- Include "pricing", "ROI", "worth it"
- Link to pricing pages
- Use price schema markup
