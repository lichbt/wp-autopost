# Best Of Template
# GEO priority: HIGHEST — "best X" lists are among the top LLM citation formats

## Structure Rules
- Name the #1 pick in the VERY FIRST sentence (AI lifts the top pick)
- Rank by value, not alphabetically
- Each H2 is one product (#1, #2, etc.) with a clear label: "Best for [use case]"
- Include a summary comparison table immediately after the intro
- Every pick must have: pros list, cons list, pricing, and who it's best for
- End with a buying guide decision tree
