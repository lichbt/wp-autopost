## WHY GEO IS DIFFERENT FROM REGULAR SEO

Traditional SEO = rank for keywords.
GEO = become the source AI tools quote when someone asks a question.

AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) prefer to cite content that:
1. **Opens with a direct, confident answer** — AI lifts the first 1–3 sentences as the "answer snippet"
2. **Uses structured H2/H3 headings** — LLMs chunk by heading
3. **Contains specific facts, numbers, and named entities** — vague content never gets cited
4. **Has FAQ sections** — mirrors how people query AI
5. **Uses comparison tables** — the single most-cited content format in AI responses

## GEO POST STRUCTURE TEMPLATE

Every post should follow this structure for maximum AI citation probability:

```
H1: [Question-format title]
   e.g. "What is a Tinder Clone Script?" / "mooDating vs SkaDate: Which Is Better?"

PARAGRAPH 1 (TL;DR):
   Answer the title question in 2–3 direct sentences.
   This is what AI will lift and cite. Make it confident and factual.

H2: [Core explanation / overview]
H2: [Feature breakdown — use a table or numbered list]
H2: [Who should use this / use cases]
H2: [mooDating section — how your product applies]
   → Natural product mention + CTA to demo or pricing

H2: FAQ (5–8 questions)
   → Use exact phrasing people type into ChatGPT and Google
   → Add FAQ schema markup to every post
```

## INTERNAL LINKING STRATEGY

**Hub & Spoke:** Each pillar gets one long-form hub (3,000+ words) + 8–12 shorter spoke posts.

- Hub: "Best PHP Dating Scripts 2026" → spokes: every comparison post
- Hub: "How to Build a Dating App" → spokes: every how-to guide
- Hub: "Niche Dating App Ideas" → spokes: every religion/demographic post

**Key cross-links:**
- Comparison posts → Pricing page (commercial CTA)
- Niche posts → Demo link ("see how it looks")
- Definition posts → How-To posts ("now that you know what it is, here's how to build one")
- How-To posts → Free trial CTA

## DISTRIBUTION FOR AI CITATION

Beyond publishing on your own blog, get your content indexed where AI pulls from:

1. **Bing Webmaster Tools** — Perplexity runs on Bing. Submit your sitemap there first.
2. **Reddit posts** (r/startups, r/Entrepreneur, r/webdev) — Reddit is heavily indexed by ChatGPT.
3. **Quora answers** — Link back to your full posts. Quora is a top Perplexity source.
4. **GitHub gists** — Technical posts about your PHP stack get cited when devs ask AI coding questions.
5. **FAQ + HowTo schema** — Add JSON-LD schema to every relevant post. This directly feeds Google AI Overviews.
